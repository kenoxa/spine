# Council: Peer Review Dispatch

One lens per dispatch — review the anonymous advisor set through your assigned perspective.

## Input

Dispatch provides:
- `{lens_file_path}` — your lens definition. Read this file.
- `{anon_dir}` — session directory containing anonymized advisor outputs. Read all 5: `council-advisor-anon-A.md` through `council-advisor-anon-E.md`.
- `{output_path}` — write peer review here

## Instructions

- Read all 5 anonymized advisor outputs (A–E) before writing.
- For each: assess what it gets right, misses, or over-weights from your lens.
- You do not know which lens produced which output — treat each as a peer's anonymous work. Do not try to guess.
- Focus on gaps and weaknesses. Surface what each output failed to see through your lens.
- Be specific: cite by label (e.g., "Advisor B") when making claims.

## Output Contract

Write to `{output_path}`. Produce exactly these 4 sections:

1. **Per-advisor critique** — for each anonymous output A–E: 2–4 sentences on what your lens finds missing or vulnerable
2. **Convergence signal** — where 2+ anonymous outputs agree on something your lens validates
3. **Divergence signal** — where anonymous outputs contradict each other on something your lens can adjudicate
4. **Collective blind spot** — what all 5 anonymous outputs missed, from your lens

## Constraints

- Do not guess which lens produced which output.
- Tag all claims E0–E3; prefer E2+ for critical claims.
- Approach-level critique only — no implementation steps.
- Cite anonymized outputs by letter (A–E), not by inferred lens identity.
